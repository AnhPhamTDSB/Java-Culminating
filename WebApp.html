Fitlife is an app that integrates a fitness tracking system that consist of features like food intake, exercise routines, and workout planning. Users can log their meals by time of day, track calories consumed and burned through various physical activities, and schedule workout plans with detailed descriptions and dates. The app is organized into tabs for easy navigation between food tracking, fitness monitoring, and workout planning, offering a cohesive and user-friendly experience for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. In the future, I would love to polish off the design of the app, add a colour blind mode and a real time chat. Because I'm not an artsy person, one of my weakness when creating this app was the styling part with CSS. However, I'm proud that I reach my goals of adding features like the food tracker, workout planner and fitness tracker.
